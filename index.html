About the Artist

Hello! I'm
Naviah Johnson

I am the photographer behind the lens and a lover of books—everything self-help, psychology, and philosophy (and the odd romance🫣). I live for productive days listening to my favorite songs and dancing in my kitchen!

The Journey

Originally from Prince Albert, SK, and now in Calgary, creating has always been my language—from pencil drawings and poetry to singing. When I found photography, I knew it was the perfect career path for my ambition to do things my way, regardless of the cost.

Entrepreneurship

A 9-5 never appealed to me. I ventured through Shopify and dropshipping before a high school photography class changed the trajectory of my life. From Skills Canada to producing a hockey documentary, I was thrown headfirst into video production.

"Today, I offer social media management, coaching, and brand photography. My goal is to make you look more professional online through content that actually makes sense for you."

I am constantly inspired by how passionate business owners are and I absolutely want to connect for that reason alone!
